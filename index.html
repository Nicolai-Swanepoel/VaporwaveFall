This game is pretty easy: Simply select a style (Really Easy or Hardcore Mode) and characters in contained in little statue heads will fall down. Save them by pressing the correct chracters on your keyboard before it hits the ground.
After each save, the game gets harder and the statue heads will fall faster. Hardcore Mode will also punish you when you pressed the wrong character, taking away a life. When playing this game over and over, you might even improve your typing skills.
